Mapping the findings

During this master's I did a very interesting project about misinformation on social media within our chosen target group, teenagers between 14 and 18 years old. During this project we found many good and important insights through our research, a workshop we did and testing our prototype. Our final concept is, in my opinion, very good based on all the findings we made. We have developed a concept that you can use on various social media platforms, such as TikTok and Instagram.

Unfortunately, it is not possible that TikTok and Instagram will actually use this. As a result, our research does not go beyond the walls of the AUAS and I think that is a shame. That is why I would like to create a document in which I visually visualize the process with the findings (and possibly design choices). As a result, teenagers also find it 'fun' to view instead of reading a document with only words (because teenagers prefer not to read, we have noticed ;)).
